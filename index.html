General Rules & Format

  1. 👥 All registered teams (max 24) are divided into groups with 4 teams in each group.
  2. 🏸 Each team plays one game against every team in its group. Rankings are based on points/scores.
  3. 🔢 All games rally point to 21 (maximum 30 points – ends when a team reaches 30).
  4. 🥇 Winners get 2 points; losers get 0. Scores are recorded.
  5. 🚫 No-show results in a walkover win for the present team (2 points awarded).
  6. ⚖️ If points are tied, score differential decides ranking.
  7. 🏆 Top teams from each group qualify for quarterfinals, ranked by performance.
  8. ⭐ Top 2 or 3 remaining teams overall may qualify as wild card entries.
  9. 🔨 Referees' decisions are final.
  10. 🤝 Players must not argue with opponents. Violators may be disqualified.
  11. 🎯 Group games are 1 set. Playoffs are best of 3 sets.
  12. ⏱️ Water breaks: 1 minute mid-set; 3 minutes between sets. No other breaks allowed.
  13. ↔️ Teams switch sides after 10 points in group games and during 3rd playoff sets.
  14. 🔄 Substitutions allowed only during group stage in case of injury. No subs in playoffs.
